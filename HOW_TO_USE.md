## ðŸŽ¯ **How to Use - Step by Step**

### **First Time Setup:**

1. **Add Your Subjects**
   - Go to "Subjects" tab
   - Click "Add Subject"
   - Enter: Name (e.g., "Calculus") and Description
   - Repeat for all your subjects

2. **Schedule Your Exams**
   - Go to "Exams" tab
   - Click "Add Exam"
   - Fill in: Exam name, Subject, Date, Priority
   - Add all upcoming exams

3. **Generate Study Plan**
   - Go to "Study Planner" tab
   - Set your daily study hours (e.g., 5 hours)
   - Choose start and end dates
   - Click "Generate Plan"
   - The system will create a smart schedule!

4. **Track Your Progress**
   - Go to "Progress Tracker" tab
   - Complete today's tasks
   - Record actual hours studied
   - Add notes about your progress
